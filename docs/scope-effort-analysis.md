# Scope & Effort Analysis

> Comparative effort analysis across three delivery models for the Agentforce for Marketing solution template — covering research, architecture, 24-component implementation plan, design review, and two branded HTML presentations.

## What Was Delivered

Three deep research streams (Data Prism, Metadata Studio, Semantic Data Model), a full Agentforce solution architecture with 3-layer NL-to-SQL engine, a 24-component implementation plan (v2) with complete Apex/metadata code, an 11-gap design review from v1→v2, and two polished brand-compliant HTML presentations (scrollable + interactive deck).

## Delivery Summary

- **Research Streams**: 3
- **Deployable Components**: 24
- **Design Gaps Found & Fixed**: 11
- **Branded Presentations**: 2
- **Plan Versions**: 2
- **Lines of Code/Config**: ~1,900

## Effort Breakdown by Work Category

| Work Category | 100% Manual | 50% Hybrid (LLM in IDE) | MeshMesh + Human Guidance |
|---|---|---|---|
| Technical Research (Data Prism, SDM, Metadata Studio) | 12–16 hours | 6–8 hours | ~45 min |
| Solution Architecture Design | 8–12 hours | 4–6 hours | ~30 min |
| Implementation Plan (24 components w/ code) | 24–32 hours | 10–14 hours | ~1.5 hours |
| v1→v2 Design Review (11 gaps) | 6–8 hours | 3–4 hours | ~20 min |
| Branded HTML Presentations (×2) | 12–16 hours | 4–6 hours | ~15 min |
| Iteration & Revision Cycles | 8–12 hours | 4–6 hours | ~15 min |
| **Total** | **70–96 hours** | **31–44 hours** | **~3.25 hours** |

## Translated to Business Cost

| Metric | 100% Manual | 50% Hybrid | MeshMesh |
|---|---|---|---|
| Calendar Time | 2–3 weeks | 1–1.5 weeks | 1 session (~3h) |
| Senior SA/Architect Hours | 70–96h | 31–44h | ~3.25h |
| Estimated Cost (@ $175/hr SA rate) | $12,250 – $16,800 | $5,425 – $7,700 | ~$569 |
| Rework Risk | High — gaps found late | Medium — some AI hallucination | Low — iterative review built-in |
| Knowledge Capture | Tribal / undocumented | Partial — scattered prompts | Full — plan + artifacts + presentations |

## Impact

- **Time Saved vs Manual**: 96%
- **Time Saved vs Hybrid**: 91%
- **Cost Saved vs Manual**: ~$15K
- **Deliverables Created**: 8 artifacts

## Assumptions

**Manual baseline** assumes a senior Solutions Architect or Technical Architect familiar with Agentforce, Data Cloud, and Marketing Cloud — including time for documentation research, architecture whiteboarding, writing Apex/metadata code, creating branded collateral in Figma or HTML, and peer review cycles.

**Hybrid baseline** assumes the same architect using an LLM-assisted IDE (e.g., Cursor) for code generation and research acceleration, but still manually orchestrating the workflow, context-switching between tools, and assembling deliverables.

**MeshMesh baseline** reflects actual execution time in this session with human guidance on scope decisions and iterative review requests.
